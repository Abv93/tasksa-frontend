Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

TaskSA (Pty) Ltd ("TaskSA", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("POPIA"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you register, book a service, list a service, or use TaskSA, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your physical address or service area (province, city, suburb)
  • Profile photos and listing photos you upload
  • Booking details, messages sent through the platform, and reviews
  • Payment information processed through our payment provider (we do not store your card details)
  • Device information and push notification tokens if you enable notifications

2. Why We Collect This Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect clients with service providers, shops, and accommodation hosts
  • Process bookings, orders, and payments
  • Send booking confirmations, updates, and notifications via email, push notification, or SMS
  • Enable communication between clients and providers
  • Improve our platform and respond to support queries
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Who We Share Your Information With

We share limited information as follows:

  • Other users: when you book a service or message a provider, they see your name, contact details, and message content necessary to fulfil the booking
  • Payment processors: PayFast processes card payments on our behalf; we do not have access to your full card details
  • Service providers: we use SendGrid for email delivery, Twilio for SMS, and Cloudinary for image hosting
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted password storage, secure HTTPS connections, and access controls to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal, accounting, and dispute-resolution requirements.

7. Cookies and Tracking

TaskSA uses local browser storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the platform.

9. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights under POPIA, contact us at hello@tasksa.co.za.
